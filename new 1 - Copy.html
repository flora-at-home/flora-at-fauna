Benefits of Hydroponics

Why Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is science not magic. It needs to be understood properly. The disadvantages need to be addressed properly to get a good yield.

Benefits

  • As there is no soil requirement, growing can be done practically anywhere


  • Water can be reused efficiently


  • You can control the nutrient usage and avoid any wastages


  • The plants can be totally pesticide free by providing a sterile environment


  • The system is highly space saving when compared to the yield it can produce


  • Laborious tasks like Tilling and Fumigation are not needed


  • As you can control the environment in which the plant grows, the plants can be grown at any time of year


  • The entire process from sowing, growing to harvesting can be a relaxing family hobby that is highly eco-friendly


    • Constraints

  • The cost for the initial setup of the system is higher than conventional cultivation
  • More time and dedication is required as constant supervision is mandatory for a good yield
  • Any manual mistakes affect the plants much quicker as there is no soil acting as a buffer
  • Power Outage can be a big hurdle where electrical appliances are used
  • Waterborne diseases can spread more quickly
